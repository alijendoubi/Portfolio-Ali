Process

How I approach product work.

I work best on projects that need both technical build quality and practical business understanding.

01

Map the workflow

I start with the real process: people, handoffs, bottlenecks, and where mistakes or delays are actually happening.

02

Design the system

I turn that workflow into product structure, data models, service boundaries, interfaces, and operational safeguards.

03

Ship usable software

I prioritize reliability, clarity, and actual adoption so the delivered system can be used daily without heroics.
